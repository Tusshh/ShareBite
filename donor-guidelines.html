Donor Guidelines

Thank you for choosing to support the community by donating surplus food. Please follow these guidelines to ensure safety and quality:

  • ✔ Donate only fresh, hygienically prepared, and properly stored food.
  • ✔ Ensure food is packed securely to avoid leakage or contamination.
  • ✔ Label items clearly with preparation date and type (veg/non-veg).
  • ✔ Do not donate expired, spoiled, or opened packaged food.
  • ✔ Maintain cleanliness during food handling and transportation.
  • ✔ Coordinate with volunteers/NGOs for smooth pickup and delivery.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team.
